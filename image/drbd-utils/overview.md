## About drbd-utils

drbd-utils provides DRBD (Distributed Replicated Block Device) user-space utilities for managing DRBD resources in
clustered storage environments. These utilities enable configuration, monitoring, and administration of DRBD devices,
which provide high-availability block-level replication across network-connected storage systems.
